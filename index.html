Voice - Controlled Music Player
Control music with your voice!

Instructions:
To Play a Song - say "PLAY (song-name) and/or (artist name)."
eg. "play billie jean / play Michael Jackson"
OR
"PLAY billie jean michael jackson"

To Pause - say "STOP."
Navigate videos with "NEXT" and "BACK".
"RESUME" to continue playing.


Or you can search for a channel.
